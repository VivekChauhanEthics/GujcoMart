About Ethics Retail Chain Services Pvt Limited

At Ethics Retail Chain Services, a proud venture under the umbrella of Ethics Group of Companies, we stand as a beacon of retail excellence, ethical practices, and unwavering commitment to cooperative values. As the governing company behind the esteemed GUJCO brand, we are honored to collaborate with GUJCOMASOL, shaping the future of retail in Gujarat.

Our Retail Expertise:

Innovative Solutions for Dynamic Retail

Ethics Retail Chain Services brings forth a wealth of retail expertise, rooted in innovation, sustainability, and a deep understanding of the cooperative ethos. With a dynamic approach to retail management, we focus on driving growth, fostering efficiency, and delivering transformative solutions.

Commitment to Cooperative Values:

Building communities, enriching lives

Our commitment to cooperative values is at the heart of our operations. We believe in fostering mutual benefits, not just for stakeholders but for the entire community. By integrating ethical practices into our retail model, we aim to create a positive impact on the lives of farmers, entrepreneurs, and consumers alike.

Excellence in Retail:

Setting standards, defining excellence

Ethics Retail Chain Services sets the gold standard in retail operations. With a vast network of 482 strategically located warehouses and a logistical prowess that dispatches over 72,000 line items daily, we ensure that every product reaches its destination with precision and efficiency. Processing an average of 7,500+ invoices per day and managing a diverse portfolio of 20,000+ products, we stand as a reliable partner for businesses across India.

Sustainability Initiatives:

Caring for the Planet, Serving Generations

In our pursuit of excellence, sustainability takes center stage. We actively engage in initiatives that contribute to a resilient India. Through our extensive reach covering 19,000+ pin codes, we establish a broad and inclusive distribution network, ensuring that our sustainability efforts positively impact every corner of the nation.

Mutual Benefits for Stakeholders:

Empowering Entrepreneurs, Creating Value

Ethics Retail Chain Services is not just a retail solution provider; we are a catalyst for economic empowerment. Through our collaboration with GUJCO, we empower entrepreneurs with innovative FINTECH solutions, facilitating access to funds for sustained business growth.

Where Values Meet Retail Excellence

Explore the collaborative journey of GUJCO and Ethics Retail Chain Services. As we redefine retail in Gujarat, we invite you to be a part of an experience that goes beyond transactions, embracing values, sustainability, and a commitment to excellence.
