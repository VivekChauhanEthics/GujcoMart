Welcome to GUJCO
Empowering farmers, enriching communities

At GUJCO, we are not just an organization; we are the cornerstone of Gujarat's agricultural prosperity. As the Gujarat State Co-operative Marketing Federation Ltd. (GUJCOMASOL), we stand as the Apex Cooperative Marketing Federation, deeply rooted in the agro-centric community, with an annual turnover exceeding Rs. 4764.36 crores.

Our Commitment to Agriculture:

Driving Agro Prosperity, Enhancing Lives

GUJCO is at the forefront of agro inputs, farm output procurement, and a myriad of services provided through an extensive cooperative network across the state. Our business extends beyond profit, focusing on fostering agro-economic growth and elevating the lives of our farmers.

Core Business Offerings:

Quality Inputs, Sustainable Outputs

Our core business revolves around providing top-tier agricultural inputs such as Seeds, Pesticides, Fertilizers, and value-added Consumer Goods. We're not just in the business of trading agriculture commodities and warehousing; we are in the business of empowering farmers to achieve unparalleled success.

Farmer-Centric Initiatives:

Educate, Empower, Enrich

Beyond the transactional, GUJCO is dedicated to the holistic well-being of our farming community. We actively educate farmers with advanced package practices, keeping them updated on technological advancements in farming. Through awareness programs and doorstep services, we connect with the heart of agriculture - the soil.

Cooperative Values:

Service Over Profit, Community Over Commerce

Being a cooperative, we prioritize service over profit. Our ethos revolves around the prosperity of the farming community, strengthening our connection with the soil, and understanding the unique needs and concerns of our farmers. We have a direct connection with more than 6000 Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (PACS) and over 40 lakh farmers in the state.

Enter the GUJCO Experience:
Empowering entrepreneurs, building super stores

In collaboration with GUJCOMASOL, Ethics Group proudly introduces Ethics Retail Chain Services Private Limited - a dedicated entity committed to offering franchises to entrepreneurs. Our common brand, "GUJCO," stands as a symbol of cooperative ethos and the expertise Ethics Group brings to the retail chain industry.

VISION & MISSION

We aspire to be a market leader, driving innovation and fostering growth in the agricultural sector. Through co-operative networking we are committed to offer quality products that provide value for money. We also constantly strive to empower and highlight the efforts of farmers in our daily consumption cycle while simultaneously driving sustainability and standing upright with our motto of sirf sasta nahi, kifayati!
